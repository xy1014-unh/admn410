Our Policies

Financing


Payment Options

We gladly accept the following forms of payment:


  • Credit Cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal
  • Durham House of Pets and Unleashed by Durham House of Pets Gift Cards and eGift Cards
  • Pals Rewards Dollars, in $5 increments

Sales Tax

By law, we must collect applicable sales tax for orders shipping to states where we have retail stores. Shipping fees may also be subject to tax in certain states. The sales tax for an order will be displayed in the Order Summary once the shipping address has been entered.


Sales tax does not apply to purchases of Donations, Gift Cards and eGift Cards.


Tax Exemption

For those who qualify for a tax exemption, please submit your request for exemption by faxing your tax certificate, resale certificate, or other required documentation to (858) 784-3489. On a cover sheet, be sure to include your order number. A Tax Specialist will review your certificate once it has been received. We will notify you by email within five (5) business days if your request has been approved.


Your order will initially be taxed. Once we've received your request and it has been approved, we will process a refund back to the original form of payment for the applicable amount. An automated email will be sent as confirmation that the refund has been processed. You may also log in to your account to view the adjustment on the Order Details page. Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to appear on your banking statement.


Return


Two Easy Ways to return your purchase


1. Return to a store (It’s Free!)

Your local Durham House of Pets or Unleashed by Durham House of Pets store will gladly accept the return of your Durham House of Pets.com order within 60 days of the initial shipment date. To find the store nearest you, check out our Store Locator.


In order to process your return in-store, please be prepared to show one of the following:


  • Store Return Receipt – print at home or have ready on your smart phone when you reach the register. A link to print or view your Store Return Receipt is found in your Order History with your account. Once you've located your order, select "View Details" next to the corresponding order details to access your receipt. On the Durham House of Pets App you can easily view it under the My Orders section.
  • Purchase Confirmation Email – print at home or have ready on your smart phone when you reach the register.

For returns made after 60 days, or if you do not have a receipt, you will be issued an in-store only merchandise credit for the listed store price or allowed to exchange the product for something else.


If exchanging for a different item, you will receive credit for the amount paid as listed on your Store Return Receipt.


Personalized items, prescription food or pharmacy items cannot be returned to a store. Please see below for details.


Orders placed online using your PayPal account may be returned to any Durham House of Pets or Unleashed by Durham House of Pets store for a refund on your credit card, with receipt, or may be exchanged.


2. Ship your items back

Please note that shipping charges are non-refundable. However, should you need assistance, please contact Customer Service.


Durham House of Pets.com orders can also be returned by mail within 60 days to one of our warehouse/fulfillment centers. A refund for the item will be issued once it has been received.


Return to below, or the noted Return Address on your Packing Slip:


Durham House of Pets.com

1 Elliot Way,

Manchester, NH 03103


At our discretion, a refund may be issued without requiring a return of the product. In these situations, please note that we do not take ownership of the refunded item.


Refunds for orders placed online using your PayPal account and returned to a Durham House of Pets.com warehouse will be applied back to your PayPal account.


Merchandise credit will be emailed to the account owner for returns made after 60 days to our warehouse.
